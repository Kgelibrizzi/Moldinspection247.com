How It Works & What to Expect – Mold Only

We provide three levels of Assessment. Each investigation is conducted by a Certified Industrial Hygienist (CIH), Licensed Mold Assessor, AND a Risk Manager!

Why is this important? The highly technical CIH training ensures environmental health competency. Florida licensure ensures things are done legally (critical when dealing with insurance). The risk manager puts it all into a real-world context—so you understand practical options and avoid upsells or blind spots.

Each inspection starts with a pre-visit questionnaire so we can understand your issues, history, and concerns. Begin the process now by requesting a questionnaire: Kenneth@geriskmanagement.com.

We offer 3 levels of service:

Standard Assessment Includes:

  • Pre-visit Questionnaire / Interviews / Incident History
  • Physical Inspection & Equipment Use
  • Industry Protocols & Accountability Standards
  • Air and/or Swab Mold Sampling + Accredited Lab Analysis
  • Assessment Based on Mold Guidelines & Standards
  • Detailed Report with Protocols & Recommendations

Level 1 – Basic (Starts at $149)

  • Pre-visit Questionnaire / Interviews
  • Travel or Virtual as needed
  • One-room visual inspection & on-site verbal estimate
  • No sampling included

Level 2 – Standard (Starts at $349 + Lab Fees)

  • Everything in Level 1
  • Multi-room inspections & intrusive inspection as needed
  • High-powered lighting & photo documentation
  • Specialized equipment: moisture meters, IR imaging
  • Up to three mold samples (air/surface)
  • Chain-of-custody & lab drop-off
  • Quantitative or qualitative mold spore analysis
  • Risk interpretation, regulatory info, remediation protocol
  • Direct insurance billing (if applicable)

Level 3 – Premium (Starts at $500 + Lab Fees)

  • Everything in Levels 1 & 2
  • Any extra services necessary for full certified product
  • Detailed identification of mold origin, extent & amplification
  • Customized services available—just ask!

Note: Above pricing is for typical residential buildings. For commercial inspections or large homes, please contact us directly for a custom quote.
