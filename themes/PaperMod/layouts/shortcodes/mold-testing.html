Mold Testing & Evaluation Services

Our mold assessments are built on certified experience and evidence-based methods. Whether you’re investigating a specific odor, visible mold, or water damage—or preparing for a property transaction—we provide detailed, defensible evaluations aligned with Florida regulations and national best practices.

What Our Mold Testing Includes

  • Moisture intrusion evaluation with digital metering
  • Infrared (IR) thermal scanning to identify hidden anomalies
  • Air sampling (spore trap) and surface sampling (swabs or tape lifts)
  • Assessment of building materials, occupant risk factors, and prior history
  • Detailed reporting with lab analysis and recommendations

When Should You Test for Mold?

Testing is recommended when:

  • Mold is suspected but not visible
  • You’re buying or selling property and want documentation
  • A prior remediation project requires post-remediation clearance
  • Occupants report unexplained health symptoms or odors

We Are Not a Remediation Company

We perform independent mold testing and do not profit from cleanup. That means you get objective, certified results—not a sales pitch. We work for you, not a contractor.
