More Than Mold? Advanced Testing for Health-Sensitive Homes

Looking Beyond Visible Mold?

Most inspectors stop at what they can see. We don’t. Our Certified Industrial Hygienist (CIH) offers advanced environmental testing that can detect hidden or overlooked contaminants affecting your indoor air quality and health.

Our Advanced Home Testing Services Include:

  • ERMI Testing – DNA-based mold screen (MSQPCR lab method) for evaluating home moldiness vs. national data
  • VOC Screening – Volatile organic compounds and chemical gases (paints, cleaners, off-gassing materials)
  • Bacterial Sampling – Especially useful post-flood or for homes with suspected water damage
  • Dust Characterization – Lab analysis of microscopic dust contents: fibers, fragments, spores, pollen, etc.
  • Colorimetric Tube Kits – Easy do-it-yourself air test kits for specific gases (e.g., formaldehyde, ammonia)

Why This Matters for You:

  • Peace of Mind – Know what’s in your air and dust — even if you don’t see visible mold
  • Helpful for Doctors – Useful for families with asthma, immune issues, unexplained symptoms
  • Smarter Decisions – Don’t guess on remediation, chemicals, or source tracking

Certified Oversight:

All advanced testing is overseen by a CIH — Certified Industrial Hygienist — the gold standard for environmental health expertise.
